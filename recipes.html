Apple Oat Bread

Ingredients:

Apple juice 3/4 cup
Bread flour 2 cups
Sugar 2 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Butter 1 Tbsp
Oats 1/4 cups
Cinnamon 1/3 tsp
Dry yeast 1 tsp
Dried Apple 1/4 cups

Methods:

  1. Adding all the ingredients into your breadmaker in the order given.
  2. Then select the "Basic Bread" button.
  3. When the breadmaker finished it work you are ready to serve.
